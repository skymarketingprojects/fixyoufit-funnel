About Dr. Vaishnavi Suresh(PT)

BPT, MPT (Ortho), CDTP, DKF, MFCT, BFR-T, FRCPT , Women’s health therapist

She earned her bachelor’s degree (BPT) from KIMS Bangalore. She completed her master’s degree in orthopedics, manual therapy (MPT) from Manipal University. She pursued her course in Sports physiotherapy from Core Physio India. She is a certified Women's health therapist and personal counselor by the guidance of V care women. She holds a Diploma in Kinetic Flossing (DKF). She is also specialized as a Myofascial Cupping Therapist (MFCT), Certified Dynamic Taping Practitioner (CDTP), Certified IASTM practitioner, and Blood Flow Restriction Trainer (BFR-T). Her latest is the completion of Fellowship in radiology course (FRCPT) from International Institute of Digital Teachings.

She has a flexible nature of therapy from using tools to just the hands to achieve the recovery of the patient. Her keen interest is to deal with orthopedic cases using manual therapy. Her practice cycles from evidence-based practice to practice-based evidence techniques. Her sports physiotherapy skills were enhanced with her postings as an on-field physiotherapist. She has been treating pregnant women in private practice since the beginning of her career that led her to explore more in women's health and get the expertise professionally. She now has her personal brand Womb Wellness for treating women of all ages with unique services given 1st time in South India.
